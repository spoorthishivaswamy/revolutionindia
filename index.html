About JATAYU

While the entire nation is outraged with never ending violence against women and children, we at Revolution INDIA are constantly working towards putting an end to this menace. We request all the women out their to help us with this survey for us to come up with a better solution. This project is about building an IT solution to help women in distress and adopt a holistic approach in resolving issues that women face in all walks of life.
